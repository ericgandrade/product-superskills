# abx-strategy

Build Account-Based Everything (ABX) GTM strategies for complex B2B sales. Covers ICP scoring, account tiering, buying committee mapping, messaging architecture, pipeline acceleration plays, and expansion campaigns — designed for companies with fewer than 500 target accounts, $100K+ deals, and 6+ month sales cycles.

## When to use

- Building GTM strategy for enterprise B2B products with limited addressable markets.
- Creating ICP scoring models and Tier 1/2/3 account segmentation.
- Mapping buying committees (economic buyer, champion, influencer, blocker) and per-persona messaging.
- Designing pipeline acceleration plays for late-stage stuck deals.
- Running ABX expansion campaigns into an existing customer base.
- Aligning sales and marketing around key accounts.

## What is included

- `SKILL.md` — Full ABX strategy workflow: ICP scoring, account tiering, messaging architecture, campaign design, pipeline acceleration, and field enablement.
- `templates/` — Supporting templates for ABX execution.
- `evals/evals.json` — 3 test cases covering GTM strategy, pipeline acceleration, and expansion campaigns.
- `evals/trigger-eval.json` — 20 trigger accuracy queries (10 should-trigger / 10 should-not-trigger).

## Typical invocation

- "Build me an ABX GTM strategy for our enterprise SaaS — $180K deals, 200 target accounts, 8-month sales cycles."
- "Design an ICP scoring model and account tiering for our B2B sales team."
- "Our late-stage pipeline is stalling — create an ABX acceleration play for 12 stuck accounts."
- "Build an expansion ABX campaign to sell our new module to our existing 150 enterprise customers."
- "Map the buying committee for a deal with 7 stakeholders and create per-persona messaging."
