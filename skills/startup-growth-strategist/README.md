# startup-growth-strategist

Unified business authority for market validation, financial modeling, competitive strategy, and Go-To-Market execution.

## When to use

- Validating a startup idea or business model.
- Calculating market size (TAM/SAM/SOM).
- Projecting revenue and unit economics (LTV/CAC).
- Designing GTM strategies and Bet Boards.
- Analyzing competitive differentiation.

## What is included

- `SKILL.md` — Integrated growth strategy workflow covering TAM/SAM/SOM, unit economics, competitive analysis, and GTM execution
- `templates/` — Market sizing methodologies and industry templates (SaaS, marketplace, developer tools)
- `examples/` — Worked examples of SaaS and marketplace models
- `evals/evals.json` — 3 realistic test cases with assertions for trigger accuracy and workflow correctness
- `evals/trigger-eval.json` — 20 queries (10 should-trigger / 10 should-not-trigger) for description optimization

## Typical invocation

- "Use startup-growth-strategist to calculate the market size for our new app."
- "Model our financial projections and unit economics for the next 3 years."
- "Design a GTM Bet Board for our product launch."
- "Analyze the competitive differentiation for our B2B SaaS startup."

## What's New in v2.0

- **Progress Tracking** — 4-phase gauge bar (Market Sizing → Unit Economics → Competitive Analysis → GTM Strategy) displayed during execution
- **EVals** — `evals/evals.json` with 3 realistic test cases; `evals/trigger-eval.json` with 20 queries (10 trigger / 10 no-trigger) for description optimization
- **Standardized description** — SKILL.md description updated to Anthropic skill-creator format
